A colorful, interactive, and engaging HTML/CSS website showcasing the most iconic and beautiful tourist destinations in Taiwan.

- Jiufen Old Street
- Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall
- Sun Moon Lake

- Taroko Gorge National Park
- Alishan Mountain Resort
- Keelung Night Market
- Pingxi Sky Lantern Festival

- Jinguashi Old Street

🎨 **Design Features**
- Vibrant gradient backgrounds

- Interactive destination cards with hover effects
- Professional navigation bar
- Beautiful hero section
- Engaging fun facts section

📸 **Each Destination Includes**
- High-quality images from Unsplash
- Exact GPS coordinates
- Location information
- Interesting historical facts

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download all files (index.html, styles.css, script.js)
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. Click "Start Exploring" or use the navigation menu
4. Scroll through the destinations
5. Click on any destination card to see more details

## 🎯 Destinations Overview

### 1. 🏮 Jiufen Old Street
- Historic alleyway with traditional shops
- Famous for taro balls and tea houses
- Over 900 years of history

### 2. 🏛️ Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall
- Iconic white monument in Taipei
- Hourly changing of the guard ceremony
- Beautiful Liberty Square

### 3. 🌊 Sun Moon Lake
- Taiwan's largest freshwater lake
- Perfect for cycling and hiking
- Famous Oolong tea region

### 4. 🏢 Taipei 101
- Tallest building in Taiwan (508m)
- 360° panoramic views
- Iconic New Year fireworks

### 5. ⛰️ Taroko Gorge National Park
- UNESCO World Heritage Site
- Dramatic marble cliffs
- 15+ hiking trails

### 6. 🚂 Alishan Mountain Resort
- Historic forest railway
- 2,000-year-old cypress trees
- Famous "Sea of Clouds"

### 7. ⛩️ Keelung Night Market
- Taiwan's most famous night market
- Over 200 food stalls
- Operating since 1945

### 8. 🏮 Pingxi Sky Lantern Festival
- Magical sky lantern experience
- Annual festival in February
- Symbol of wishes and dreams

### 9. 🪨 Yehliu Geopark
- Unique geological formations
- Famous "Queen's Head" rock
- Best viewed at low tide

### 10. 🏔️ Jinguashi Old Street
- Historic gold mining town
- Japanese-era architecture
- Mountain scenery with tea plantations
